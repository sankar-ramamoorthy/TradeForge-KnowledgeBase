# M10E Opportunity Workspace Stabilization

The final M10E Opportunity refinements establish a trader-facing workspace pattern:

- instrument identity is explicit and persistent
- primary surfaces answer operator questions before exposing projection mechanics
- internal ontology remains canonical, but operator-facing copy is translated where cognition is the task
- missing advisory context is recovery-oriented
- conditional reasoning is expressed in trader language while preserving canonical scenario semantics
- advisory reasoning prompts guide discretion without becoming recommendation or lifecycle authority

This closes the Opportunity refinement sequence without changing event truth,
lifecycle ownership, or replay authority.
