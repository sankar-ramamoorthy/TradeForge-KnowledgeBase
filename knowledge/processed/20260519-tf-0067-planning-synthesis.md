# TF-0067 Planning Synthesis - Review Assistance

Review assistance should interpret existing review reflection artifacts, not become review authority. The service should produce source-linked advisory artifacts through the TF-0065 boundary and leave review completion, event writing, and behavioral intelligence out of scope.
