# TF-0068 Planning Synthesis - Advisory Provenance Tracking

Advisory provenance tracking should preserve generated AI advisory artifacts as non-canonical records. The store boundary must be queryable and replay-relevant, but it must not become an event ledger or lifecycle authority.
