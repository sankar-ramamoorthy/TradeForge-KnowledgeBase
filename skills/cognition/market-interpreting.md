# Skill: Market Interpretation

## Purpose

Defines how raw market data is converted into structured context.

---

# Core Principle

Market data is:
> raw observation, not meaning

Meaning must be constructed.

---

# Interpretation Layers

## 1. Raw Data
- prices
- volume
- indicators
- news events

## 2. Structured Signals
- volatility shifts
- regime changes
- liquidity conditions

## 3. Market Context
- macro environment
- risk-on/off regime
- thematic flows

---

# Hard Constraint

Codex MUST NOT:

- treat raw data as actionable insight
- infer trade decisions directly from data
- skip interpretation layers

---

# Interpretation Rule

All market understanding must pass through:

1. normalization
2. structuring
3. contextualization
4. scenario impact mapping

---

# Anti-Pattern

Bad:
> “Price increased so this is bullish”

Correct:
> “Price movement contributes to a potential regime shift scenario but requires validation through broader context”

---

# Final Principle

Markets are interpreted systems, not direct signal generators.
