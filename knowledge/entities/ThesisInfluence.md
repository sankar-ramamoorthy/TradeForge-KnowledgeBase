# ThesisInfluence

ThesisInfluence is qualitative advisory metadata describing how an
AdvisoryInterpretation relates to an existing or potential thesis.

Allowed meanings include supporting, weakening, conflicting, mixed, neutral, and
unknown.

ThesisInfluence is not thesis revision authority. It does not mutate a
TradeThesis, change confidence, approve a plan, or promote a lifecycle stage.
